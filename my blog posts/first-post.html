Now i know some people are going to burn me at the stake just for mentioning docker but truly its the only way i develop anymore

Dev containers have changed my workflow completely and allow me to port things between machines at a much faster rate


Not only that, but I am able to share these workflows with other developers and get them up and running with minimal effort


Now I will say that the drawbacks for initial setup are a little much but once you get into a flow, it gets much easier.
